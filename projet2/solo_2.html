Ben Foulds

Ben Foulds is an international vocal & guitar cover artiste, performing an acoustic tribute to music
of all eras. Ben is based in the West Midlands, but is currently working regularly nationwide.
With 12 years of performing experience ranging from his work as a solo musician through to taking leading roles in musical theatre stage shows.
